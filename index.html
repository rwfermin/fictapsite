What We Do

FICTAP is a 19-year old reputable organization and renowned for bringing entertainment to all Filipinos from Batanes to Tawi-Tawi! We are the largest non-profit organization with a 1,000-strong membership of small and medium cable enterprises from all over the country.

Our 1,000-strong membership is composed of small and medium cable enterprises that have embraced the expansion from delivering traditional cable TV into telecommunications and value-added services.
